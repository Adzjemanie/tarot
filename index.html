Tarot Detail Cutter — Major Arcana + Minor Suits

Upload an image per section. The app automatically cuts “interesting” detail crops and lays them out as tarot cards. Hover a card for its meaning. Each card can be downloaded as a ZIP (PNG + meaning text), and you can also download all cards in one ZIP.

How it works
The app analyses the image for variance, edge energy and colour saturation, then selects the most visually interesting regions. Major Arcana: 22 cards. Minor Arcana per suit: 14 cards (Ace through 10, Page, Knight, Queen, King).

Each downloaded PNG has the number and title baked into the image. The ZIP contains all cards in subfolders per suit, plus a description file per card.
